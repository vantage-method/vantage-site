Who I Am

Built for the challenges expert-led businesses face.

I'm Ken Freire, and I've spent 15 years in the trenches with founders, consultants, coaches, and service providers who are great at what they do — but struggle to scale without sacrificing their quality, culture, or sanity.

I've worked with businesses generating anywhere from $500K to $10M+ annually, helping them professionalize their operations, build predictable sales systems, and develop the leadership infrastructure needed to grow without founder burnout.

My approach is grounded in clarity and simplicity. I focus on building systems that work — not tactics that fade. Whether you're stuck at a revenue plateau, drowning in client delivery, or struggling to turn authority into consistent pipeline, I help you build the operational backbone that turns expertise into scalable, predictable growth.

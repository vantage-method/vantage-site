Who I Am

Growth strategy that moves the needle.

I'm Zach Gray — fractional CMO, growth strategist, and partner at Vantage Method. I've spent my career helping founders who've outgrown word-of-mouth marketing build the systems they need to scale.

I've generated over $40M in revenue for direct clients and coached more than 1,400 businesses through workshops and keynotes. My focus isn't running campaigns — it's identifying the key levers that transform a business from founder-dependent to systematically scalable.

I work with founders who are great at what they do but stuck at a growth ceiling. Whether it's unclear messaging, a leaky funnel, or an offer that doesn't convert — I find the bottleneck and build the system to fix it.
